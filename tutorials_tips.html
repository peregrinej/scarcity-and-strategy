Strategy Tips

Improve your gameplay with these strategy tips:

  • Balance Resources: Ensure that you allocate resources evenly across industries to avoid over-reliance on one sector.
  • Monitor Efficiency: Keep an eye on your efficiency percentages and adjust your strategy to maximize productivity.
  • Plan for Scenarios: Be prepared for unexpected events that may impact your resource availability. Have a backup plan to reallocate resources quickly.
  • Optimize Resource Use: Use resources in a way that maximizes output while minimizing waste. Consider the opportunity cost of your decisions.
  • Learn from Feedback: Use the feedback provided by the game to identify areas for improvement and refine your strategy.
