Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I allocate resources? Use the input fields to enter the number of resources you want to allocate to each industry and click "Allocate."
  • What happens if I exceed resource limits? The game will alert you if you exceed the available resources, labor hours, or capital. Adjust your inputs to stay within limits.
  • How is efficiency calculated? Efficiency is calculated based on your resource usage relative to the total available resources, labor, and capital.
  • Can I play with friends? Yes, you can choose multiplayer mode to play with friends. Each player needs to create an account and join the game session.
  • How do I reset the game? To reset the game, log out and log back in, or refresh the game board to start a new session.
