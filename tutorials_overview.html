Game Overview

The "Scarcity and Strategy" game is designed to teach players about fundamental economic concepts through interactive gameplay. Players take on the role of economic decision-makers, allocating resources between different industries to maximize efficiency and achieve economic goals.

The game involves managing resources such as labor, capital, and raw materials to produce goods and services. Players must navigate various challenges and scenarios that reflect real-world economic conditions.

Key Concepts Covered:

  • Scarcity and Opportunity Cost
  • Production Possibility Frontier (PPF)
  • Supply and Demand
  • Market Equilibrium
  • Economic Efficiency
