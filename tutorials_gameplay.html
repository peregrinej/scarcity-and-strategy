Gameplay Instructions

Follow these instructions to play the "Scarcity and Strategy" game:

  1. Allocate Resources: Use the input fields to allocate resources to different industries. Each resource type has a limit (e.g., labor hours, capital).
  2. Submit Allocations: Click the "Allocate" button to submit your resource allocations. The game will process your inputs and display the results.
  3. Analyze Results: Review the production results and efficiency percentages. The game will provide feedback on your resource management decisions.
  4. Adjust Strategy: Based on the feedback, adjust your strategy and reallocate resources to optimize production and efficiency.
  5. Complete Objectives: Continue playing until you achieve the game objectives or reach the end of the session. Track your progress and aim to improve your scores.
